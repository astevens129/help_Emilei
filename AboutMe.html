About Me

Hi! My name is Emilei, it's pronounced like “Emily”. I moved to the Central Indiana region a year ago and am originally from Peru, Indiana. I take great pride in being a self-made, self-taught business owner—an achievement fueled by the encouragement of those around me.
Art has always been a passion of mine, and I've long known I wanted to pursue a creative career. Helping people is another core passion, which recently evolved into helping businesses improve their image, communication, and media presence.
In January 2025, after extensive research, I launched my business.
More recently, I began studying technology with a focus on web design so I can one day expand my offerings to include tech services. You can follow my journey and get updates on Facebook.
