About Me

Hi! I'm Emilei — pronounced like “Emily.” I recently relocated to Central Indiana from my hometown of Peru, Indiana.

I'm proud to be a self-made, self-taught business owner, and I owe much of my growth to the support of my personal and professional community. Since childhood, I've had a deep love for art, and I've always known I wanted to turn that passion into something meaningful.

Over time, I’ve discovered a strong interest in helping businesses refine their brand, improve their messaging, and elevate their digital presence. This passion led me to launch Emilei's Art in January 2025.

More recently, I began studying web design and technology with the goal of expanding my services to include tech-based solutions for clients. I’m excited to continue learning and will be sharing updates on my journey via my Facebook page.
